How We Work

Our Creative Process

01

Concept

Every project begins with an idea — researching the subject, gathering references, and defining the visual direction.

02

Sketch

Rough layouts, storyboards, and initial frames — getting the vision out of our heads and onto the screen.

03

Animate

Frame by frame, week by week — bringing each element to life with patience, craft, and creative instinct.

04

Refine

Reviewing, polishing, and reflecting — making sure every frame sings before the final export.
